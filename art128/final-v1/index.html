Sure Shot Cafe

1997

Good Food, Good Coffee, Good People

Sure Shot Cafe does not accept being a "specialty" shop. Providing not only good food, good coffee, but also good people (we take pride in our employees and the customers we have cultivated). Originally known as Village Espresso, our current owner came down to get a cup of java with her dad one day in '97 after hearing thru the grapevine that there was a cafe she had never heard of for sale. "What a nifty place this is.." agreed the two. Before you know it...BADA-Bing, BADA-Bang! Sure Shot Cafe was born in 1998.

Mahalo Customers

Our customers come from not just the Makiki area, but from around the island, and even the country. We serve all demographics; from young high school students, to retired folk looking for a relaxing afternoon cup of joe. We focus on every individual who walks through our doors. It is because of our loyal (and new) customers that we are able to be here for you. Thank you!
